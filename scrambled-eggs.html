Scrambled Eggs

Recipe

Duration: 10min

Difficulty: Easy

dinner_dining

Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation

Step1:

Crack eggs into a bowl and beat well with a fork or whisk

Step2:

Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter

Step3:

Pour in beaten eggs and cook, stirring constantly, until set but still moist

Step4:

Season with salt and pepper to taste

Step4:

Serve hot with toast or any desired toppings
