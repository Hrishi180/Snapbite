Aloo Paratha

Recipe

Duration: 25mins

Difficulty: Medium

dinner_dining

Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Wheat flour
  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • Salt
  • Ghee or Oil

Preparation

Step 1:

In a large bowl, mix together the flour, mashed potato, cumin seeds, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt

Step 2:

Add enough water to make a soft and pliable dough

Step 3:

Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth

Step 4:

Divide the dough into equal-sized balls

Step 5:

On a lightly floured surface, roll each ball into a thin circle, about 6 inches in diameter

Step 6:

Heat a tawa or griddle over medium heat

Step 7:

Place the rolled dough circle on the tawa and cook for 30-40 seconds on one side

Step 8:

Spread a little ghee or oil over the surface of the paratha

Step 9:

Flip the paratha and cook for another 30-40 seconds or until golden brown spots appear on the surface

Step 10:

Spread a little ghee or oil over the other side of the paratha

Step 11:

Flip the paratha and cook for another 30-40 seconds or until golden brown spots appear

Step 12:

Repeat the process with the remaining dough balls

Step 13:

Serve hot with raita, pickle, or chutney. Enjoy!
