Dosa

Recipe

Duration: 10min (excluding prep)

Difficulty: Easy

dinner_dining

Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Salt
  • Ghee

Preparation

Step 1:

Wash the rice and urad dal together and soak in water for at least 6 hours or overnight

Step 2:

Drain the water and grind the rice and dal together in a blender to a smooth batter

Step 3:

Transfer the batter to a large bowl, add enough water to make a batter of pouring consistency, and add salt to taste

Step 4:

Cover the bowl and let the batter ferment for 6-8 hours or overnight

Step 5:

Heat a non-stick tawa (griddle) over medium heat. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the center of the tawa and spread it out in a circular motion to form a thin and even layer

Step 6:

Drizzle a little ghee around the edges and on top of the dosa

Step 7:

Cook until the edges start to brown and the surface of the dosa is dry and crisp, about 2-3 minutes

Step 8:

Fold the dosa in half and transfer to a serving plate. Repeat the process with the remaining batter

Step 9:

Serve hot with chutney and sambar. Enjoy!
